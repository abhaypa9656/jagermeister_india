About Us

Born in 1934 in Wolfenbüttel, Germany, Jägermeister has become one of the world’s most iconic herbal liqueurs. Crafted from a closely guarded recipe of 56 carefully selected herbs, roots, fruits, and spices sourced from around the globe, Jägermeister represents a bold balance of tradition, precision, and character. Our story began with Curt Mast, who perfected the recipe and named it “Jägermeister,” meaning “Master Hunter.” Inspired by nature and craftsmanship, the brand’s legendary stag logo pays tribute to Saint Hubertus, the patron saint of hunters — a symbol of strength, respect, and authenticity that continues to define us today. Every drop of Jägermeister undergoes a meticulous production process. The natural ingredients are ground, steeped, and matured in oak barrels for up to a year before being carefully filtered and blended. The result is a smooth, complex spirit best served ice-cold at -18°C — a ritual that has become part of our global identity. From underground music scenes to international nightlife, Jägermeister has always stood alongside those who move culture forward. We support creativity, independence, and the spirit of bringing people together. More than just a drink, Jägermeister is a symbol of bold taste, shared moments, and unforgettable nights.
